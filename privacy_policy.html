Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 28, 2025

At Night Tales, accessible from https://apps.apple.com/app/6744857585, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by Night Tales and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

NightTale is a text reading app that does not collect any personal information. The app does not gather data such as your name, email, or phone number, ensuring your privacy while using it.

2. Data Usage

Night Tales does not track user activities or collect any analytics. It functions offline, and no personal information is required for app usage. The app solely provides text-based content for reading public domain fairy tales and mythologies.

3. In-App Purchases

Night Tales offers in-app purchases for unlocking premium stories and accessing additional features like bookmarks. These transactions are handled through the Apple App Store's secure payment system. Apple may collect personal and financial data during this process, which is governed by Apple’s Privacy Policy.

4. No User Data Storage

We do not collect or store any personal information or usage data from our users. Your reading activity, preferences, or any other personal information is not stored in our system.

5. Third-Party Services

Night Tales does not use any third-party services that require the collection of personal information or data analytics. The app does not include advertising, external links, or embedded third-party content that collects user data.

6. Children’s Privacy

Night Tales is intended for a general audience. However, we do not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from children under the age of 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately. Parents can contact us if they believe their child has shared personal information through the app.

7. Data Security

Since Night Tales does not collect or store personal data, there are no security risks regarding personal data storage. However, the app may utilize Apple's standard security measures for transactions and in-app purchases through the App Store.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. When we do, we will post the updated policy in the app and on this page. Please review this policy regularly for any changes. The effective date of the most recent update will be indicated at the top of the page.
